About Us

We are passionate about the commercial property & casualty business.

We are a trusted technology partner and advisor helping insurance organizations transform their underwriting operations with a modern AI-driven digital underwriting platform.

Who We Are

  • Founded in 2016 “By Underwriters, For Underwriters”
  • Purpose-built for commercial business
  • Customers of all sizes, including three of the top 10 commercial carriers
Vision

Transform the insurance industry through data, discovery & decisioning ​
Solution

Frictionless Commercial Underwriting system powered by Patented AI

At Convr®, we set out to solve

the last big problem of commercial insurance

To underwriters, the challenge to assemble structured and unstructured submission data, access a multitude of first and third party risk data for accurate classification and selection adherence, ask the right questions to ensure suitability, and understand the relative ranking of each risk within its class has continued to defy consistency and frustrate the overall underwriting process.

Solutions to these problems have been hampered by their relatively late placement in IT roadmaps and the associated financial burdens already been borne by earlier system upgrades and replacements – many of which extended well-beyond their budgets and scheduled implementations.

And we solved the problem

Our customers have documented impressive, measurable results after engaging Convr and you could too.

Founded by underwriters for underwriters we are:

  • An AI underwriting platform that digitizes and fuses submissions with the best sources of data to surface underwriting insights, business classification and risk scores ​
  • Obsessed with organizing the submission process and all the related data inputs to surface the most relevant and important information about each risk​
  • Committed to reducing the hours spent gathering submission information and prepping it for analysis

Our customers achieve faster and more complete judgements on risk fitness and pricing​ in addition to increased submission efficiency and capacity. We truly help to improve the lives of underwriters, producers and customers who, at the end of the day ​are just trying to deliver and secure the best insurance coverage at the most reasonable rate.
